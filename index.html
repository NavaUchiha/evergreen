This is where I write down the ideas I don't want to forget — worked out slowly, in my own words — and then come back to them a few times until they stick. Nothing fancy. Just a quiet place to learn something once and actually remember it.

The trick is simple — revisit each idea on day 1 day 4 day 7 and by then it's yours.
write it

In your own words

Explain an idea the way you'd tell a friend — the intuition, a real example, why it works. If you can write it plainly, you understand it. Everything you write stays saved.

revisit it

A gentle nudge

Come back on day 1, then 4, then 7. Remembered it? Lovely. Forgot? No guilt — it just circles back to today and you try again. That's how memory is supposed to work.

keep it

It doesn't fade

Once an idea has survived all three visits, it's yours for good — evergreen. Back the whole thing up any time and carry it to another device.
